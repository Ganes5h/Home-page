Founder & Former MLA Parshurambhau Nandihalli: Mr. Parshurambhau Nandihalli was born on 7 August 1930 at Yellur, Karnataka. His father's name is Bharmaji , Mother's name Ningubai. Parshurambhau Nandihalli is a founder of a renowned school "Shivaji Vidhyalaya" in his community. Shivaji Vidhyalaya was the first school in yellur. Parshurambhau Nandihalli is a prominent figure in the field of education, having founded a highly successful school that continues to impact students' lives today. The name Parshurambhau Nandihalli is synonymous with excellence in education, thanks to his visionary leadership as the founder of a renowned school.
